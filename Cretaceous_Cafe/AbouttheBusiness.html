Achievements

Cretaceous Cafe was to be the three-star dining restaurant for Jurassic Park. It was located somewhere between the Dilophosaurus and Triceratops Paddock, the restaurant's name was probably an allusion to these animals. Construction was said to begin in November of 1989, but never occurred.

Loghan Hornor

Loghan Haharnor came from Clovis, CA. He is a young, up and coming cook who went to Stanford to research biology, but left to pursue his dream of one day becoming an owner of a restaurant. He has been here at Jurassic Park for 3 years and has been an important part of park associates.

Alain Richard

Alain Richard, a world renowned French chef, was to be the Head Chef of the restaurant.

Gavin Sestson

Gavin originates in Clovis, CA. He is a newly learned chef learning new skills constantly with expertise in culinary arts and designs. He designs many designs that make their way from the kitchen table to your dining table.
