Achievements

Cretaceous Cafe was to be the three-star dining restaurant for Jurassic Park. It was located somewhere between the Dilophosaurus and Triceratops Paddock, the restaurant's name was probably an allusion to these animals. Construction was said to begin in November of 1989, but never occurred.

Loghan Hornor

Loghan Hornor came from Clovis, CA. He is a young, up and coming cook who went to Stanford to research biology, but left to pursue his dream of one day becoming an owner of a restaurant. He has been here at Jurassic Park for 3 years and has been an important part of park associates.

Alain Richard

Alain Richard, born in the picturesque town of Lyon, France, was destined for greatness in the culinary world. His innovative approach to traditional cuisine and his ability to blend diverse flavors garnered attention and rising reputation. He is the founder of our magnificent business.

Gavin Stetson

Gavin originates in Clovis, CA. He is a seasoned chef learning new skills constantly with expertise in culinary art and designs. He articulates many designs that make their way from the kitchen to your dining table. Perchance.
