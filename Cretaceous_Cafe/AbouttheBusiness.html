Achievements

Cretaceous Cafe is a three-star, full-service dining restaurant for Jurassic Park. It is located between the Dilophosaurus and Triceratops Paddock. The restaurant's name is an allusion to these animals. Construction began in November of 1989, and completed in an amount of months.

Loghan Hornor

Loghan Hornor came from Clovis, CA. He is a young, up and coming cook who went to Stanford to research biology, but left to pursue his dream of one day becoming an owner of a restaurant. He has been here at Jurassic Park for 3 years and has been an important part of park associates.

Alain Richard

Alain Richard, born in the scenic town of Lyon, France, was destined for greatness in the culinary world. His innovative approach to traditional cuisine and his ability to blend diverse flavors garnered attention and rising reputation. He is the founder of our magnificent business.

Gavin Stetson

Gavin originates in Clovis, CA. He is a seasoned chef learning new skills constantly with expertise in culinary art and designs. He articulates many exsquisite meals that make their way from the kitchen to your dining table. Perchance.
